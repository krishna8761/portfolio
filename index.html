FAQ
Answers before you ask.
How long does a project usually take?
+

Most single-page or small business sites take 1-2 weeks from kickoff to launch. Larger projects with custom features or app builds can run 4-6 weeks. You 'll get a clear timeline before we start.

Do I need to pay upfront?
+

Yes. I work on a no-advance, no-work basis — a portion of the project cost is due before work begins, with the rest on delivery. This keeps things fair and clear for both of us.

How many revisions do I get?
+

Every project includes a set number of revision rounds, agreed upfront in your project agreement. Extra rounds beyond that can be added if needed.

Do you work with clients outside India?
+

Absolutely. While most current clients are based in India, Monark Studio is built to take on international work — remote-first, async-friendly, and comfortable working across time zones.

How do we communicate during the project?
+

Mainly WhatsApp for quick updates, with email for formal deliverables and agreements. You 'll always know exactly where your project stands.
